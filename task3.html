2. Relationship of loan default risk and loan amount/payment

p2 <- ggplot(data1,aes(x=loan_default,y=loan_amount)) +
  geom_boxplot(width=0.5, show.legend = FALSE, fill =  '#ffcc99')+
  labs(title = "loan amount VS loan default") +
  xlab("loan default") +
  ylab("loan amount")
p3 <- ggplot(data1,aes(x=loan_default,y=loan_payment)) +
  geom_boxplot(width=0.5, show.legend = FALSE, fill =  '#ccccff')+
  labs(title = "loan payment VS loan default") +
  xlab("loan default") +
  ylab("loan payment")
grid.arrange(p2, p3, ncol=2)

From the left box plot, we can conclude that account with higher loan aomunt is more likely to default. From the right plot above, people with higher loan payment is more likely to default.
